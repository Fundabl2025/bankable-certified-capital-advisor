Frequently Asked

Honest answers to the questions most prospects do not ask out loud.

Why become a Bankable™ Certified Capital Advisor?
Because it turns your existing relationships, network, or career into a recurring advisory practice. The Capital Readiness Advisory category is forming right now. Profit First built a $30M+ network on professional certification. Bankable is doing the same thing for capital readiness — a larger market, a more urgent problem, and a more defensible designation. You attract higher-value clients, differentiate your services, create recurring advisory revenue, and build a more stable, profitable practice.
What's the difference between Capital Connector and Certified Capital Advisor?
Capital Connector is the entry tier — $7 to start a 14-day trial, then $49/month. You can refer business owners who need funding and earn referral income immediately. You're not a certified advisor and you don't run engagements. It's the on-ramp into the profession. Once you're ready to run the methodology yourself and own the client relationship, you upgrade to Certified Capital Advisor and unlock the full 7 Cs curriculum, Bankable IQ™, HuslFi™, the lender network, and the official designation.
What are the main benefits of becoming a Bankable™ member?
You attract higher-value clients, differentiate from generic brokers and consultants, create recurring advisory revenue (not one-time deal commissions), and build a real practice on a defensible methodology. Members also get the official certification seal, the full ecosystem of tools (Bankable IQ™, HuslFi™, Bankable Tax Strategy™, Bankable Capital™), a private community of peer advisors, and direct access to Kevin Murphy and the Bankable™ leadership team.
Is certification required to start earning?
No. You can start earning as a Capital Connector on Day 1 by referring business owners into the Bankable™ ecosystem. Certification is required if you want to run advisory engagements yourself, deliver readiness reports, charge clients directly for the Bankable methodology, or earn the official Certified Capital Advisor designation. Most members start as Connectors, earn while they study, then upgrade to Certified when they're ready.
What support do members receive?
Every member receives onboarding, structured curriculum, the Bankable™ resource library, a private off-social community forum, group accountability pods, monthly group training, daily office hours, and ongoing mentorship from your assigned Bankable™ Guide. Certified Capital Advisors and above also get monthly 1-on-1 Zoom calls with their Guide. Master Architects get direct mentorship from Kevin Murphy through the Inner Circle Mastermind.
Is this just another broker training program?
No. Broker training teaches you to push applications. The Bankable™ Certified Capital Advisor program trains you to diagnose, organize, optimize, and prepare businesses for institutional capital — before applications are ever submitted. It is a professional advisory designation, not a referral certification.
Do I need a finance, accounting, or lending background?
No. The program is designed for anyone willing to learn the skill — career-changers, aspiring advisors, entrepreneurs, and people with no prior finance experience. The 7 Cs framework, the Lender Lens, and the software-supported workflows give you the complete structure regardless of your starting point. Experienced funding pros, CPAs, and consultants simply move through the curriculum faster.
What is the Founder-to-Advisor Pathway? Do I have to be a Bankable client first?
The Founder-to-Advisor Pathway is the strongly recommended (but not required) route where business owners use the Bankable™ system to get their OWN business institutionally ready and funded first, then turn what they lived through into a profession by helping others. It produces the most credible advisors in the field. You can also enter the certification path directly without being a client first — the choice is yours.
Why 7 Cs when lenders use 6 Cs?
The lender's 6 Cs is an EVALUATION framework — what banks use to judge a business. Bankable's 7 Cs is a READINESS framework — what advisors use to BUILD the business before evaluation. Six of our Cs mirror the institutional 6 Cs directly. The 7th — CLARITY — is the prerequisite no lender framework names but every lender assumes you have. CONFIDENCE is the emergent outcome when all 7 are aligned.
How long does certification take?
The core curriculum is built for focused completion in 30 days. Most advisors complete all 9 modules, pass the final exam, and begin running paid engagements within 30 days of enrollment. The structure is intentional — you start earning fast, you don't drag through a year-long course.
What software and tools come with the certification?
Certified advisors receive access to the Bankable IQ™ platform, readiness scoring engine, advisor scripts and conversation frameworks, document collection workflows, packaging templates, the community network, and ongoing coaching cadence. HUSLFi™ integrations and broader ecosystem layers are available as the practice scales.
How do Certified Capital Advisors actually make money?
Multiple revenue paths: readiness engagements (one-time or recurring), packaging fees, funding coordination commissions, ongoing monitoring retainers, and partner referrals. Module 09 lays out a 12-month practice growth plan with realistic revenue benchmarks at each stage.
What is the relationship between Bankable, Bankable IQ, and HUSLFi?
Bankable™ is the institutional readiness ecosystem and methodology. Bankable IQ™ is the software platform that delivers assessments, scoring, dashboards, and continuous monitoring. HUSLFi™ is the financial intelligence infrastructure that powers bookkeeping, forecasting, and operational analytics for the businesses you advise.
Is there a firm or group licensing option?
Yes. CPA firms, bookkeeping practices, agencies, and business service providers can license the Bankable™ Certified Capital Advisor designation for their teams. Firm pathway includes group onboarding, white-label deliverables, and dedicated implementation support.
